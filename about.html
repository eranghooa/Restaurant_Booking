Our Story

Who We Are

Born from a love of French tradition

Maison Lumière was founded in 2008 by Chef Édouard Renard, who trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens in Lyon and Paris before bringing classical French cuisine to Singapore. The name — meaning "House of Light" — reflects his belief that great food illuminates the senses.

Nestled in the heart of the city, our dining room seats just 48 guests, ensuring that every table receives the attention and care it deserves. We source our produce daily from local markets, supplemented by carefully selected imports — Brittany sea salt, Valrhona chocolate, and aged Comté from the Jura mountains.

Nearly two decades on, Maison Lumière remains a family endeavour. Édouard's daughter, Amélie Renard, joined the team in 2019 as Sommelier, curating a cellar of over 300 labels spanning the Loire Valley to Burgundy.
