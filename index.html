The Menu

❀

Each dish honours the Japanese philosophy of shun — the peak of seasonal perfection — composed by our itamae with ingredients sourced daily from Tsukiji and Kyoto's finest producers.

前菜 Starters

冷製茶碗蒸し

Chilled Chawanmushi

Silken steamed egg custard infused with dashi and aged mirin, crowned with Hokkaido sea urchin and a whisper of yuzu zest

S$28

刺身盛り合わせ

Sashimi Moriawase

Daily selection of seven seasonal cuts — bluefin tuna, yellowtail, red snapper, sweet shrimp — arranged on hand-carved Kishu cedar with wasabi from Shizuoka

S$68

鮭の西京焼き

Sake Saikyo-yaki

Hokkaido king salmon marinated 48 hours in Nishiki Kyoto white miso, grilled over binchōtan charcoal until lacquered, served with pickled ginger shoots

S$42

握り Nigiri Signatures

大トロ握り

Otoro Nigiri (2 pcs)

Premium fatty bluefin tuna belly from Ōma, Aomori — pressed over hand-formed Koshihikari rice seasoned with aged red vinegar and brushed with our house nikiri soy

S$58

馬糞雲丹

Bafun Uni (2 pcs)

Golden bafun sea urchin from Rishiri Island, Hokkaido, set directly on warm rice with a single grain of Okinawan sea salt — nothing more, nothing less

S$72

のどぐろ炙り

Nodoguro Aburi (2 pcs)

Flame-kissed blackthroat sea perch — Japan's most prized fatty white fish — lightly seared to coax its oils, finished with ponzu and micro shiso

S$54

主菜 Main Courses

和牛しゃぶしゃぶ

Wagyu Shabu-shabu

Paper-thin A5 Kagoshima Kuroge Wagyu swirled tableside in a 48-hour Rishiri kombu and katsuobushi dashi, with ponzu, sesame tare, and hand-cut somen noodles

S$148

鱧の炙り焼き

Hamo Aburi-yaki

Seasonal Kyoto pike conger, bone-scored with 120 cuts per inch by our itamae, flame-seared and draped in umeboshi plum, perilla flower, and chilled ponzu

S$88

黒毛和牛フィレ

Kuroge Wagyu Fillet

A5 Miyazaki Kuroge Wagyu tenderloin, grilled to order over hardwood binchōtan, rested and served with fresh wasabi, sea salt, and smoked dashi butter

S$198

甘味 Desserts

抹茶パフェ

Matcha Parfait

Ceremonial-grade Uji matcha ice cream layered with hand-rolled shiratama mochi, slow-simmered Tamba azuki, roasted houjicha granola, and kuromitsu black sugar syrup

S$22

桜餅

Sakura Mochi

Delicate pink Domyoji-style rice parcels enclosing koshi-an sweet red bean paste, wrapped in a brine-pickled Oshima cherry leaf — the very taste of Japanese spring

S$18

黒蜜わらび餅

Kuromitsu Warabi-mochi

Trembling bracken-starch jelly — silkier and more ethereal than ordinary mochi — blanketed in roasted soybean kinako powder and hand-pressed Okinawan black sugar syrup

S$16
